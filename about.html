About Fort Thomas Donuts
Bartholemuel Lockord, the visionary founder of Fort Thomas Donuts, was a true culinary maverick with an unyielding passion for all things doughy and delightful. With a twinkle in his eye and a secret family recipe passed down through generations, he set out on a mission to bring the joy of freshly baked donuts to the town of Fort Thomas.

Bartholemuel Lockord's journey took many years of small jobs and unwavering work to gain a name for himself. He worked tirelessly, pouring his heart and soul into his work, and in 1949 his buisness would be founded, leaving in impact on Fort Thomas.
